About Us

Our Story

Our story is one of passion for nature and a deep-rooted commitment to environmental conservation. It all began with a simple idea: to create a user-friendly website that would empower people around the world to connect with the natural world by identifying and learning about plants. As a team, we embarked on this exciting journey to develop a plant identification website that would make the wonders of the plant kingdom accessible to everyone.

Objective

The primary goal is to offer a reliable tool that enables users to identify plants accurately. This can be accomplished through image recognition technology, user-contributed photos, or expert verification.Build and maintain an extensive database of plant species, including scientific names, common names, descriptions, images, habitat information, and other relevant details.

Approach

Implement machine learning algorithms to improve the accuracy of plant identification. Trained the system with a diverse dataset of plant images for better results. Include features for user-generated content, such as plant photo sharing or plant scaning using mobile camera.
